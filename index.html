Roadmap.

Welcome to the «Rich Dwarves Tribe».

As you will discover below, our goal is to build a tribe that brings together the most passionate collectors and investors.
1. Our Goal

Our top priority is to deliver high quality NFTs for the community. We have chosen to work with artists who, like the dwarves and the rest of the team, works from the depths of the mine. This keeps us focused on our main goal : to deliver high quality NFTs. Each dwarf follows the same creative process with high standards. So you can enjoy and flex on social media your digital artworks and watch them appreciate in value.

2. Our Vision

Our years of hard work allowed us to grow several successful businesses in sectors such as e-commerce, real estate and crypto investments. We realized that Blockchain technology would change the world as we know it. Our mindset combined with the fact that we all started our first businesses in our teenage years allowed us to evolve as fast as the world around us these last 10 years. The world is changing and this is just the beginning. Our goal is to bring together in the same universe a tribe of discreet hard workers and genius
entrepreneurs who will contribute to change the world. Who better than dwarves to represent us? This NFT project is not just another tick off the list of what we have already accomplished. It’s the result of years of work developing skills and building a team of enthusiasts. The « Rich Dwarves Tribe » could not be more representative of who we really are on a daily basis. We unite our skills with a common goal: to imagine, create, develop and undertake with those who are like us.

3. The Community

Without you, nothing is possible. Despite the fact that there is a hierarchy in the dwarves tribe, we wanted everyone to be on the same level. The « Rich Dwarves Tribe » isn’t just a collection of high quality artistic NFTs. It is also and above all a useful project that brings together investors, entrepreneurs and celebrities who shares the same values of fellowship with a common goal. That’s why, owning at least one NFT from the « Rich Dwarves Tribe » automatically grants you a spot in our tribe. So we can go big together.

4. The Rewards

This first chapter is about MO$$O’s clan. Once all the dwarves of the clan reunited we’ll be able to retrieve the clan’s great treasure. Loads of rewards will also be distributed to the most committed tribe’s members. Each new chapter will progressively reveal new treasures of
ever-increasing value. We’re not talking about mugs or t-shirts, but « treasures ». Some of them might change your life (the rewards will be revealed when we hit 30k members on the Discord). That’s why this project is only for collectors and investors who like to see their efforts rewarded.

5. The Future

Each clan has a treasure that can be retrieved in the real world. How can you take part in the adventure ? Once you’ll own at least one Rich Dwarf from the first clan, you will be able to join the second chapter. You will then be among genuine collectors and investors who will have perceived the true value of the Rich Dwarves Tribe. We believe that top tier entrepreneurship is art in its own form. This is why this project’s goal is to bring together a community of passionate entrepreneurs, collectors, investors and celebrities around a common artistic project. We made the choice to use 100% of the royalties for the growth of the project and to increase the value of the rewards.

6. The Metaverse

Once all the clans are reunited and their great wealth retrieved, the tribe will be able to celebrate at the tavern in The Sandbox or Decentraland (we’ll let you decide). Beyond having a place to meet, we really want to enter the future by investing in the creation of new projects where each of your NFTs will have its use, a stacking utility will be developed, dwarves are well known miners so each NFT will mine coins for you. An incubator will be created in the Metaverse to bring together the new generation of investors, entrepreneurs and celebrities. This will allow us to unite around common values. You will be able to develop your projects with a dedicated fund and take part in the development of new ones.

FAQ

What is The « Rich Dwarves Tribe »?

Chapter 1 is about the first clan : legendary MO$$O’s. It features a collection of 4999 unique and high quality 3D NFTs. More information about the following chapters once the first one is sold out.

How many dwarves are available?

The first chapter regroups 4999 high definition 3D dwarves NFT, stored as ERC-721 Tokens on the Ethereum Blockchain. All hosted in IFPS.

Why multiple chapters ?

This is a challenge that the whole team has set. Unlike many NFT projects, we wanted to create an original story that would make the « Rich Dwarves Tribe » collection meaningful. We envisioned a long-term strategy that will allow the collection to grow in value in time. As dwarves, let’s win big together.

When can I mint ?

The mint date will be announced on our Discord and pre-sales for the GoldenList will open the day before.

How can I mint?

You will need a Metamask wallet. You can check out our tutorial on Discord.

What is Metamask ?

Metamask is a crypto-currency wallet that allows you to store your Ethereum tokens. It’s with these tokens that you will be able to mint a Dwarf from MO$$O’s tribe.

How can I access my NFTs?

Once you have successfully created a Rich Dwarf NFT, you can view it by connecting your crypto wallet to OpenSea.

What blockchain do you use?

We use the Ethereum blockchain.

What are the secondary royalties?

We take 5% royalties in order to develop the next chapters and reinvest all of it in marketing.

Who is behind this project?

A team of passionate and seasoned entrepreneurs. For more details, you can discover us on Discord.

Will there be a pre-sale?

Yes, there will be a pre-sale for the members of the private GoldenList. 3000 Dwarves will be reserved for this presale.

Will there be any gifts?

Yes, but you have to be committed to the tribe to be rewarded . We will announce all the rewards on Discord.
